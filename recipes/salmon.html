Terikayi Salmon Bowl

Description

A terikayi bowl is always a go to when you want a meal to stay healthy but also having a meal packing with flavor. The spicy flavor of sriracha and the sweet/tangy flavor of the teriyaki sauce will bring out all the amazing flavors of this dish. Pairing this dish with white rice, edamame, sliced avocados and sesame seeds and green onions will soon make this your favorite dish.
