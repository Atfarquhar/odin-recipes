Cinnamon French Toast with Mixed Berries

Description

French toast is a go-to breakfast meal in the kitchen. With only a few ingredients, you can make a meal that satisfies your sweet tooth. This dish is also packing with protein. There is a yougrt topping that is made from non-fat greek yougrt and the lemon juice mixed in will create a nice light sour flavor that will go well with the mixed berries that will lay on top.

Ingredients

  • 3 slices sourdough bread
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 10 ml almond milk
  • 150g frozen mixed berries
  • 5g suagr
  • 120g non-fat yougrt
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice

Steps

  1. In a swallow bowl, mix the egg, almond milk, cinnamon and vanilla extract.
  2. Add sourdough bread to the egg mixture and coat evenly.
  3. Place butter in a nonstick pan and melt at medium heat. Once butter is melted, add bread into the pan.
  4. Cook french toast on both sides for about 2 minutes or until golden brown.
  5. While french toast is cooking place the mixed berries, sugar and a splash of water into a saucepan. Cook at medium heat and smash berries once they soften. Continue to cook unitl sauce thickens.
  6. Mix yougrt and lemon juice in a bowl and set aside.
  7. Assemble plate with french toast, followed by yougrt mxixture and topping it with the mixed berries.
  8. Enjoy!
